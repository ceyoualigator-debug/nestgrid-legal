Nestgrid — Support

Nestgrid is your household's private shared brain — capture notes, events, reminders, and facts, and share them with the people you live with. Need help? We're happy to assist.

Common questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Nestgrid works on your device by default — your captures are read locally, and nothing is sent to a server unless you turn on an optional feature (like AI extraction of a tricky note). We show no ads and never sell your data. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I invite family to my Nest?

Open Settings → Invite someone → Share invite link, and send the link to a family member. They open it on their iPhone to join. To share the household's data via iCloud, use Settings → Share this Nest (iCloud).

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Nestgrid+ is billed through Apple. Manage or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I export or delete my data?

In the app: Settings → Your data → Export my data (a full JSON copy) or Delete all data in this Nest. You can also delete the app to remove its on-device data.

A feature isn't working

Make sure you're on the latest version and that the relevant permission (camera, microphone, photos, etc.) is granted in iOS Settings. If it still doesn't work, email us at volome@gmail.com with a short description.
